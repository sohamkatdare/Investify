They were created by John Bollinger in the 1980s and provide unique insights into price and volatility. Bollinger Bands® are composed of three lines, typically calculated using a 20-day simple moving average for the middle band. The upper band is calculated by adding twice the daily standard deviation to the middle band, while the lower band is calculated by subtracting two times the daily standard deviation from the middle band. One of the most common uses for Bollinger Bands® is identifying overbought or oversold market conditions.
