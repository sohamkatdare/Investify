Why Use Investify

Investify is a comprehensive financial tool designed to empower you with the knowledge and tools to make informed investment decisions.

Our software is packed with features that provide you with valuable insights and education to navigate the complex world of investing. You can easily research stocks and access crucial investment data

such as candle graphs, open, close, low, high, and volume information

Our algorithmic stock rating score provides users with a quantitative assessment of a stock's potential, while relevant tweet and news articles, sentiment analysis, and insider trading data offer valuable market intelligence.

Our education section consists of over 20 articles on stock indicators to help users deepen their understanding of investment concepts. Users can also query using the GPT-3.5 model for information on any topic not covered in our articles.

Additionally, our paper trading functionality enables users to create an account and engage in simulated trading games with friends or other users worldwide, providing a fun and educational way to practice investing strategies without risking real money.
